3D Game

This was my second major programming project. I started on this during the first half of my senior year of high school. Having had so much success with my previous programming project, I decided to try stretching my skills to see how far I could go. I decided to try to figure out 3D graphics.


I knew how to draw lines to the screen, so I figured that would be enough to at least be able to draw wireframe objects. I started out trying to render a simple wireframe cube. My initial approach was to use all kinds of fancy trig functions to calculate a position on a screen. It worked, kind of. The result didn't quite look right, and trying to move the camera caused everything to break.


I continued messing around untill I came across a much simpler function that would make it all work.


After getting tired of messing with primatives, I tried building a first person platformer, with a grid based approach I was already familiar with.


I tried messing with height maps and slopes.


This project later helped me through calc 3, as it allowed me to visualize 3D functions.


I did very little research while building this project. Since then I have learned and worked with OpenGL and learned about the processes involed with modern graphics.
